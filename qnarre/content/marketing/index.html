Precision Distracts From Intent

Overwhelming "precision" of extensive volumes of static text can quickly obstruct the dynamic intent. Key to successful lawsuits is "seeing through" deliberate confusion. And quickly prioritizing essential narratives.

Astounding Capacity & Speed

"Producing" paralyzing amounts of confusing and misleading claims is cheap and exponentially lucrative for skilled lawyers in a digital present.

Adaptive, "deep learning" technology finally allows sifting through incomprehensibly large bodies of text, incredibly fast. Condensing the thus inferred intent into proven narratives is now possible.

Conflict Graphs As Visual Analytics

Such collections of narratives are inherently interconnected. Quantitatively measuring these "networks" gives us weighted "conflict graphs." Visualizing the graphs, or "pivoting" the underlying multidimensional data tables, opens up the possibility for insightful analytics.
